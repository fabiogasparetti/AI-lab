Artificial Intelligence Research Group

The AI‑Lab @ Roma Tre is part of the Department of Civil, Computer, and Aeronautical Engineering (DICITA) at Roma Tre University and of the Centro di Eccellenza DTC Lazio. We investigate modern AI with a focus on Large Language Models, User Modeling & Recommender Systems, Computer Vision, and Graph‑based Learning, combining rigorous research with real‑world applications for industry and public administrations.

About us

The AI program at Roma Tre University comprises a multidisciplinary group of researchers working on methods and tools for intelligent systems. Our lab embraces formal and theoretically grounded investigations, together with exploratory and empirical approaches.

The group belongs to DICITA, selected by the Italian Ministry (MIUR) as a “Department of Excellence” (2018–2022). In 2017, two faculty members joined the national AI Task Force promoted by AgID. Since 2018, the lab has been an active node of the national CINI AIIS laboratory. We are a partner of the Rome Technopole research project (Flagship 6 — AI, VR & Digital Twin for advanced engineering and aerospace).
